Introduction

Sweet Treats

Sweet Treats is a company which sells self-baked donuts and cakes and coffee and soft drinks in Pismo Beach, California. It is a little cafe near the beach where everyone, who is feeling hungry, can go in.

Target audience

They are people who already know and love the cafe, but who are looking for new specialties and offers, and people who may be passing through and are in the mood for something sweet.

Our primary task and goals are that the customer feels comfortable in our cafe and to satisfy the need that the customer feels for sweet things or a coffee.
